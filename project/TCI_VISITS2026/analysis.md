The headline comparison is matched YTD, not full 2025 versus partial 2026. Full-year 2025 is kept only as context later in the report.

- Sales are up on a matched-YTD basis, driven by a stronger Q1 and a positive same-period Q2.
- Purchase value is also up on a matched-YTD basis; Q1 was nearly flat, while same-period Q2 is ahead of 2025.
- Product classification coverage is consistent across primary, 2nd branch, and 3rd branch levels because all three come from the same matched TCI category file.
- Sales classification coverage: primary 8,434/8,849 lines (95.3%), 2nd branch 95.3%, 3rd branch 95.3%.
- Purchase classification coverage: primary 8,242/8,593 lines (95.9%), 2nd branch 95.9%, 3rd branch 95.9%.

## Data notes and assumptions

- Sales source: `_select_from_soinvhd_inner_join_soinvdt_on_soinvdt_soinvid_soinv_202605181604.csv`.
- Purchase source: `_select_from_poinvhd_inner_join_poinvdt_on_poinvdt_poinvid_poinv_202605181605.csv`.
- Product category source: `project/TCI/TCI_category_processed.csv`. Product number is extracted from `GoodCode`, for example `TCI-Z0015-300G` becomes `Z0015`.
- Primary, 2nd branch, and 3rd branch classifications are kept as combined labels when a product maps to multiple TCI classifications, preventing double-counting across hierarchy branches.
- Customer segmentation source: `account_segmentations/Account_Segmentations_2026_05_18.csv`. Sales `CustID` is matched to `Winspeed CUSTID`.
- Sales value uses line-level `GoodAmnt`, interpreted as pre-VAT sales value.
- Purchase value uses line-level `GoodAmnt * ExchRate`, interpreted as THB purchase value.
- Rows with missing category or customer-segment matches are retained as `Unmapped` rather than excluded.
- Matched YTD uses the latest loaded 2026 date for each dataset: sales through 2026-05-19 and purchases through 2026-04-27. The 2025 comparison windows use the same calendar month and day.
